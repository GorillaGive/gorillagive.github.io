Simple pricing, no surprises

During the BETA program and even after there are no access charges to the platform. After the BETA we will have simple tiers of pricing. We would like to offer a simple free tier for smaller organizations.

We will provide the most cost effective solution for payments. Currently the average cost of credit card processing is 2.9% + 0.30. The interchange rates vary between card company and type of transaction. We have no control over these prices as they are set by VISA, MC, and Amex.
